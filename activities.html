üéø True Ski-In/Ski-Out Access

Perfect for beginners after 2-3 days! The chalet has direct ski-in/ski-out access via a gentle blue run that leads right to the property. While the initial access point is a 3-minute walk to a red piste, by day 2 or 3, most beginners can comfortably ski the blue run back to the chalet door.

  • Day 1: Walk back or take the gondola down
  • Day 2-3: Most beginners can manage the blue run
  • Day 4+: Ski right to the door with confidence!
