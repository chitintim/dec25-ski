The Resort

La Tania & 3 Valleys

The perfect base for exploring the world's largest ski area

Why La Tania?

Nestled in a spruce forest at 1400m, La Tania is a charming, family-friendly resort that remains unspoilt and human-scale. It offers:

  • Direct access to the 3 Valleys (600km of pistes)
  • Quieter slopes than main Courchevel resorts
  • Authentic Alpine village atmosphere
  • Great value compared to Courchevel 1850
  • Perfect for mixed ability groups

Location Benefits

  • Ski-in/ski-out via blue run - perfect for beginners after day 2-3
  • 3 minute walk to red piste access point
  • 15 minute walk to town centre for beginners' ski school
  • Resort centre walkable (shops, restaurants)
  • No car needed during the stay
  • Direct links to Courchevel, M√©ribel & Val Thorens
